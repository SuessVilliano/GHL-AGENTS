# LIV8 OS - Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 26, 2026

## Introduction

LIV8 OS ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the LIV8 OS Chrome extension and related services.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address
- Agency/business name
- GoHighLevel location IDs

### Usage Data
- Actions performed in the extension
- API calls made to GoHighLevel
- Dashboard interactions
- Audit logs of system changes

### GoHighLevel Integration
- Access tokens (encrypted and never stored on your device)
- Contact data, conversations, opportunities
- Workflow and automation configurations

## How We Use Your Information

- **Provide Services:** To operate the LIV8 OS platform
- **AI Features:** To power brand scanning, predictions, and recommendations
- **Analytics:** To improve our services
- **Security:** To protect your account and data
- **Support:** To respond to your requests

## Data Storage & Security

- All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- GoHighLevel tokens encrypted at rest using AES-256
- Multi-tenant database with strict access controls
- Regular security audits
- Hosted on Vercel with SOC 2 compliance

## Third-Party Services

We use:
- **Vercel:** Hosting and database
- **Google Gemini AI:** Brand analysis and predictions
- **GoHighLevel API:** Your business data (via your authorization)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Delete your account
- Export your information
- Opt out of analytics

## Data Retention

- Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days
- Audit logs: Retained for 90 days for security
